Everything your hospital needs

From patient registration to discharge, Remedium covers every touchpoint in the healthcare journey.

Patient Management
Complete patient lifecycle from admission to discharge. Encrypted PHI, medical history, and insurance tracking.
Smart Scheduling
Conflict-aware appointment engine that prevents double-booking and respects doctor shifts and availability.
Pharmacy & Inventory
Real-time stock tracking, OpenFDA drug lookup, automated reorder alerts, and prescription management.
Revenue Analytics
Immutable ledger-based billing, revenue dashboards, unpaid invoice tracking, and financial reporting.
Care Monitoring
Vital signs visualization, BMI calculation, critical condition flagging, and real-time patient status.
Laboratory
Test queue management, result entry with critical range flagging, and automated lab order processing.

Security & Compliance

Built for healthcare-grade security

Every record in Remedium follows a strictly protected lifecycle to ensure data privacy and legal auditability.

PHI Encryption at Rest
Sensitive patient data (phone, email, medical history) is encrypted at the database layer using Fernet symmetric encryption.
Immutable Audit Trails
Every clinical change is tracked with timestamps and user attribution. Soft-delete ensures records are never lost.
Granular RBAC
13 distinct user roles with 8 tailored dashboards. Staff see only what they need — nothing more.
